Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 5, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service. This includes:

  • Account Information: Your email address and password for account authentication.
  • Financial Data: Expense amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates that you manually input into the application.
  • Usage Data: Basic interaction data to help us improve the application's functionality.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the expense tracking functionality.
  • To generate spending reports and financial insights for your account.
  • To secure your account and prevent unauthorized access.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored using secure encryption protocols. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Data is stored on secure servers with regular backups to ensure availability.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal or financial data to third parties. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels. Any third-party libraries used for the application's operation are strictly limited to functional requirements.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the application support channel.

Your privacy is our priority.
